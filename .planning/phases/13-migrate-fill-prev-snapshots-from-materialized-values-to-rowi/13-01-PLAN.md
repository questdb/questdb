<objective>
Ship the one-line correctness fix to `SortedRecordCursor.of()` that clears the chain when the cursor is reused (`isOpen == true`), and produce the gating D-02 investigation artifact confirming candidate (a) before any rowId code lands.

Purpose: Without `chain.clear()` on reuse, each `of()` call appends pass-1 records on top of stale chain data. Any downstream consumer that captures rowIds from a `SortedRecordCursor` chain (including the rowId rewrite in Plan 02) observes corrupted state on the second and later invocations. This is the root cause identified by RESEARCH.md §1 for the `sm_fill_prev_keysmap_driven` failure. Shipping as a standalone, bisectable commit per D-03 lets reviewers assess the fix on its own, makes it backportable, and protects the fix if Plan 02 ever reverts.

<objective>
Fix the infinite fill bug when ALIGN TO CALENDAR WITH OFFSET is used without a TO clause.

Purpose: The fill cursor's `initialize()` calls `timestampSampler.setStart(fromTs)` without
accounting for the calendar offset. Meanwhile, `timestamp_floor_utc` computes
`effectiveOffset = from + offset`, producing bucket boundaries shifted by the offset.
The sampler's grid and the GROUP BY output grid never align, so every bucket is treated
as a gap and fill rows are emitted forever.

Output: Corrected bucket alignment in the fill cursor; new tests proving finite, correct output
for offset queries.
</objective>

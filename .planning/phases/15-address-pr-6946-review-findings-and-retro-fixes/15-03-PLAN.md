- Row count: 480 rows, spanning 2018-01-01T00:00 through 2018-01-10T23:30 (10 days of data).

- `k` column: `k SYMBOL` where `k = x::symbol`; also 480 unique values.

**Key cardinality cap**: `WHERE x <= 4` reduces keys to 4 unique values (`s = '1'`, `'2'`, `'3'`, `'4'`), making output tractable.

**Bucket structure with `SAMPLE BY 5d FROM '2017-12-20' TO '2018-01-31'`**:
- Buckets start at 2017-12-20 and stride by 5 days: 2017-12-20, 2017-12-25, 2017-12-30, 2018-01-04, 2018-01-09, 2018-01-14, 2018-01-19, 2018-01-24, 2018-01-29 = 9 buckets.
- First two (pre-2018-01-01) are all-null for any data column.
- Buckets 3-5 (2017-12-30, 2018-01-04, 2018-01-09) contain data (DDL data spans 2018-01-01 through 2018-01-10).
- Buckets 6-9 (2018-01-14 onward) are all-null trailing fills.
